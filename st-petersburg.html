Craft Heritage Meets Modernist Geometry

In our Ocala workshop, we engineer wood panel assemblies, custom ceiling beam arrays, and integrated walnut wardrobes designed to fit historic framing while incorporating clean, modern lines. Our unified design-build method removes subcontractor friction on-site.
